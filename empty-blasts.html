Get Started

1. Build Your List

First, build your list of supporters who will share your content for you on their personal Twitter and Facebook accounts.

To build your list, head over to the Recruit tab.

2. Tell Them What To Share

After you've built your list of supporters, you can create posts for them to share on Facebook and Twitter. The posts can include text, links, images, or video.

To create a new blast, just hit the New Blast button above.

3. View Reports

After you send a Blast, you'll be able to see detailed reports showing you everyone who shared the post, and what kind of engagement they recieved (likes, retweetes, favorites, clicks, etc...)

The reports will automatically appear here after you send your first Blast.
